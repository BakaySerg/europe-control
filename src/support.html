How does online escrow work?

How long does the escrow process take?

While most transactions are completed within 1 to 20 business days, transaction timelines are still unique to each transaction and depend on the steps below. You can estimate the timeline of your transaction below:

01 / Account Creation and Verification:

Account creation is quick and simple. You simply fill out the required fields. However, for transactions over $3,000, all users of the transaction will need to complete an individual or company account verification. Once uploaded, your verification documents will take up to one business day. To view a list of acceptable documents for personal verification click here, or acceptable documents for a companyâ€™s verification click here.

02 / Buyer's Payment Method:

Buyers may pay for their transactions via wire, credit card, or PayPal. Wire payments are processed within 1 - 5 business days. PayPal and credit card payments are processed within one business day. Payments received during the weekend are processed on the following business day. For more information on the payment options, click here.

03 / Delivery time:

Delivery from Seller to Buyer depends on the delivery method, and whether Buyer and Seller are transacting digital goods, physical goods, or services requires all trackable and tangible merchandise be sent via a shipping method that allows online tracking and insurance.

Wire Transfers and checks sent by express deliver are received within one business day. Wire Transfers sent internationally can take three to five business days to be received. Checks sent express delivery internationally are received within two business days. ACH disbursements are completed within three business days. Checks are dependent on where the check is sent (domestic versus international).

Seller's should always use a shipping company that allows online tracking to the Buyer's address provided to ensure quick release of funds at the close of the transaction.
