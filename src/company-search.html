Reliable Company Search Services in LIVE Database

To get information on a company or business name, enter its name or number. Please define name searches as precisely as possible - you are searching the LIVE Europe Control database which is very heavily used.

There are four possible name searches:

  • [Contains all these words] searches for names containing these words (recommended)
  • [Starts with this phrase] searches for names beginning with this phrase
  • [Contains this phrase] searches for names containing this phrase.
  • The alpha sort is a string created by removing common words such as 'Ireland', 'Limited', 'The', 'And', etc., and by stripping out spaces, commas, hyphens, etc. from the company name. To perform an alpha sort search you should do the same.
  • Name searches may be narrowed by including address details.

What Information is Available Using the [Organisations & Business Names] Search?

This search provides information about companies and organizations recorded on our registers. The types of organizations you can search for include:

  • Companies
  • Registered bodies
  • Foreign companies
  • Associations
  • Managed investment schemes
  • Non-registered entities

Purchasing an Extract

When you search for a company or organization on our register, you can also purchase an extract that has current and/or historical information about the organization. This can include:

  • Past addresses
  • Previous directors
  • Former names

What Details are Available?

The information will change depending on the company or organization you are searching for. The information we provide for free includes:

  • Company or organization name
  • Unique identification number (BN, CN, RBN, RSN). Company and business name search summaries with BNs link to the Country’s Business Register
  • Type of company or organization
  • Registration date
  • Next review date
  • Location of registered office
  • Any professional licenses or registrations

Alpha Sort

You can check by using the Alphasort search, which removes punctuation, gaps between words, and common words such as "Ireland" "Limited" and "The".

The alpha sort search can be used for example where "THIS-IS. A. TEST Limited" is being searched for. Simply enter "Thisisatest" in the Alphasearch. The "- "and "." are removed. When looking for a company named "THE TRIED LOOKING & TESTED Limited", simply enter "Tried Looking Into" to find the business.

Using Company Search

The public access to the Europe Control's 'LIVE' databases provided by this site is a means by which the public can search for and view data held by the Europe Control on companies registered in the State. Customers can also request documents and reports to be returned to them by email.

When your request has been successfully processed, a confirmation receipt will be returned. This confirmation includes a receipt number that can be used in any queries to the Europe Control about such requests.

Clicking on the company number hyperlink for the company will display a list of submissions filed by that company. Select a submission or number of submission(s) and then click on the 'Request' then ‘purchase’ buttons, and the Payment Authorisation page will be displayed.
Before the Europe Control will process requests for documents it is first necessary to choose how these documents will be returned and secondly the method of payment. Payment can be made from a customer account by entering an account ID and PIN or by credit/laser card. A customer reference is taken also to allow the customer to track his/her requests. Entering all this information will ensure that requests are processed.

Please ensure that you fill in details in all 6 fields (Address 1, Address 2, City, Postcode, Country, and Phone Number) to avoid errors and proceed with payment. The input of some special characters into the fields will cause errors and prevent the credit card entry screen from loading, for instance, the phone number field does not accept brackets “(“ “)”. Any non-alphabetic/non-numeric characters apart from quotes and hyphens for the addresses should be avoided.

Auditor Search

The Register

The Register of Auditors is hosted by the Registrar of Companies and contains the names and addresses of persons or firms who have been notified to the Registrar as qualified for appointment as auditor of a company or a public auditor.

Notifications

Periodically electronic lists are submitted to the EUROPE CONTROL by the recognized bodies and these are uploaded onto the electronic register. The EUROPE CONTROL is the Competent Authority for making the Auditor Register available to the public. Each Auditor is regulated by one of the Recognised Accountancy Bodies (RAB) detailed below.

IR Auditors

There are several auditors recognized because they were authorized by the Minister in advance of the legislation regarding recognized bodies. These can be identified on the register of auditors by the Accreditation Body code Individuslly Recognised.

Auditor Search Terms

You can search for an auditor by entering a first name, surname, or firm name. If you do not find a match immediately you should try all combinations as some of the recognized bodies supply only the firm name, others only the person name and others supply both.

Using Disqualified/Restricted Person Search

The information available is an extract from the Registers of Disqualified and Restricted Persons, which are updated regularly. If you are aware of any person who continues to operate as a company director or other officer or shadow director despite being disqualified or who is acting in a capacity in breach of a restriction order you may forward this information to the Office of the Director of Corporate Enforcement at [link].

Shadow Director

A shadow director is a person who while not being listed or formally appointed as a director of a company acts in that capacity and is accepted as acting in that capacity by others. A disqualification/restriction order applying to the role of director automatically applies to that of a shadow director.

Restriction and Disqualification

Directors of a company can be restricted or disqualified from acting as an officer to a company by the High Court or a director may undertake to accept disqualification or restriction by the Office of the Director of Corporate Enforcement (ODCE) without the necessity to go to the High Court.
