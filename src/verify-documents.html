Verify Europe Control Documents

Verify Europe Control branded documents to confirm their authenticity and validity.

We provide customers with a variety of certificates and reports to confirm the results of inspections or tests we have completed. We also provide a free document verification service, for EUROPE CONTROL branded documents, which will provide clear confirmation as to whether a document is genuine or not before you rely on it for any purpose.

Combating the Counterfeiters

Every year, we check thousands of documents that are often being used to legitimately support business transactions. Although most are genuine, a small number are not. Whenever counterfeit documents are found, we take action to investigate the circumstances more fully and identify the individuals or organizations behind them. We also actively monitor third-party websites and remove adverts that make misleading or false references to Europe Control.
