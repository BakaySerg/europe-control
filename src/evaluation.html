Fairness Opinions

Corporate officers and boards of directors have a fiduciary duty to ensure a fair price in a transaction. In fulfilling their responsibilities, it may be prudent to obtain a fair opinion which is an independent and objective representation as to whether or not the consideration paid (or received) in a transaction is fair from a financial point of view.

Solvency Opinions

In transactions of high leveraging, recapitalization, and refinancing, corporate officers and boards of directors have a fiduciary duty to guard against fraudulent conveyance or improper dividend risk. In fulfilling their responsibilities, it may be prudent to obtain a solvency opinion which is an opinion as to whether a company can meet its debt obligations as they come due.
