Features

Holdback Escrows

Europe Control offers Holdback accounts to mitigate transaction risk, enabling clients to place a portion of the purchase price in escrow until the terms of an agreement are satisfied. Holdbacks offer a way for the buyer to seek claims for false representations or breaches of warranty. EUROPE CONTROL will manage all claims on the account per the agreement terms.

Good Faith Deposits

Europe Control offers Good Faith Deposits, which hold a portion of the transaction purchase price in escrow to help buyers and sellers facilitate bidding and comply with regulations. Europe Control can also distribute termination fees from the escrow funds if the transaction is canceled.

Takeover Codes

Regulations in certain jurisdictions – such as the Takeover Codes of Hong Kong and the United Kingdom – require a confirmation from the advisor in an M&A deal that resources are available to move the transaction forward. Europe Control provides the financial advisor the assurance that funds will be available and segregated throughout the offer.

Closing / Paying Agent

Closing Agent escrows – These escrows facilitate the exchange of the company stock from the seller for cash payments from the buyer.
Paying Agent services – Europe Control disburses funds to the selling shareholders directly from an escrow account.

Financing

Escrow accounts can help drip-feed long-term project financings, ring-fence financing proceeds, help in the execution of refinancing and provide a source of cash security.

Litigation

Use escrow accounts to demonstrate security for costs, ring-fence contested funds pending judgment, or provide security for class actions and appeals.

Pensions

Our pension escrow accounts help unlock negotiation with trustees, offering a robust contingent asset proposition while guarding against surplus accumulation.

Regulatory Requirements

Capital Markets

Protect investors and segregate transaction funds by safeguarding the proceeds of capital market issuances between raising and application in our Escrow accounts.
